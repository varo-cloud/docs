## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign in at [varo.cloud/auth](https://varo.cloud/auth) with your email and a one-time verification code. Your account is created automatically on first sign-in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up your balance">
    Varo Cloud is prepaid — add funds from the **Billing** page by card, Alipay / WeChat Pay, or crypto.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate an API key">
    Go to **API Keys** in the dashboard and click **Create Key**. Store it securely — it's shown only once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first generation">
    Try a model in the **AI Generator**, or call `POST /v1/generations` from your own code. Every model's API tab has ready-to-run examples.
  </Step>
</Steps>
