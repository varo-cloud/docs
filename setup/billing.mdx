Varo Cloud billing is **prepaid**: you top up a cash balance, and each generation you run — whether through the API, the AI Generator, or the Playground — is deducted from that balance at the model's listed price. There are no subscriptions or monthly plans. Everything is managed from the **Billing** section of your dashboard.

## How pricing works

Every model has its own price, listed on the [Pricing](https://varo.cloud/pricing) page and on each model's detail page. Depending on the model type, prices are quoted:

- **Per second** of generated video or audio
- **Per image**
- **Per million tokens** for language models

The exact cost of each run is recorded against your balance and visible in your billing history and generation history.

## Top up your balance

To add funds:

1. Go to **Billing** in the dashboard and click **Top Up**.
2. Choose a package — **Starter**, **Pro**, **Business** — or enter a **custom amount** (a minimum applies).
3. Select a payment method and continue to checkout.

Your balance updates as soon as the payment is confirmed. If a payment shows as **Processing**, the balance updates once the provider confirms it.

## Payment methods

Varo Cloud supports the following payment methods:

- **Card** — Visa, Mastercard, and other major cards via Stripe hosted checkout.
- **Alipay / WeChat Pay**
- **Crypto**

Receipts and invoices for completed top-ups are available from the transaction details in your billing history.

## Auto top-up

To avoid running out of balance mid-workload, you can enable **Auto Top-up**:

1. Go to **Billing** and open the **Auto top-up** settings.
2. Set the threshold — **when balance goes below** this amount, a top-up is triggered.
3. Set the **auto top-up amount** to add each time.
4. Click **Enable Auto Top-up**.

<Note>
  Auto top-up currently supports **Stripe (card)** only, and may take a few minutes to process — set your threshold with enough headroom that you don't run out before the top-up lands.
</Note>

## Usage and history

The Billing overview shows four figures at a glance:

- **Cash Balance** — what you have available to spend right now.
- **Spent this Month** — with a comparison against last month.
- **Total Top-up** — everything you have ever added.
- **Total Spent** — everything you have ever consumed.

Below that, two tabs break down the detail:

- **Billing** — every charge, with the time, style (API, Web, or Playground), task ID, status, the API key used, and the amount. You can export these records to CSV.
- **Transactions** — every top-up, with its provider, payment method, amount, processing fee, and links to the receipt or invoice.

<Tip>
  For a per-task view across models — prompts, status, cost, and outputs — see the **Generations** page in the dashboard.
</Tip>
